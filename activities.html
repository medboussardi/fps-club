In creating this club, our primary goal is to be able to participate in the MCPC (Moroccan Collegiate Programming Contest) next year, which is a problem-solving collegiate competition that focuses on programming skills. Due to the low level of students, I decided to do just some hot topics in computer science to encourage more people to get involved in the club. This can wait until next year.
